Create your virtual commemorative


Token allows businesses and individuals to create unique virtual tokens that can be collected by users, with proof of ownership on the blockchain.

Realize your ideas


Tokens may be artistic or contain media. Some may be transferable, some may be limited, and they all map to an item or experience in the real world.

Publish Faster


With this platform, anyone can release a unique digital token and receive real-time analytics on the interactions and values it has gone through. All of this can now be accomplished without needing to exhaust resources on developing and deploying your own Ethereum smart contracts.

Claim your creations


Access our mobile application to start collecting tokens. On the mobile app, users can claim tokens made by issuers all over the world.

Code to our open API


Don't want to use our mobile app or web portal? You're in luck. Use our open API to customize your T O K E N experience. Check out our docs.
