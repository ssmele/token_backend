Create your virtual commemorative


Token allows businesses and individuals to create unique virtual tokens that can be collected by users, with proof of ownership on the blockchain.

Tokens may be artistic or contain media. Some may be transferable, some may be limited, and they all map to an item or experience in the real world. Our users may receive tokens for attending a sporting event, purchasing an exclusive item, or participating in a competition.

With this platform, anyone can release a unique digital token and receive real-time analytics on the interactions and values it has gone through. All of this can now be accomplished without needing to exhaust resources on developing and deploying your own Ethereum smart contracts.
